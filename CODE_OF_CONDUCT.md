# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical issue at hand.
- Assume good faith, but do not ignore unsafe or abusive behavior.
- Share logs and configuration carefully. Remove passwords, tokens, local keys, and private network details that are not needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, private messages, or personal information.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask for the discussion to move back on topic.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to disclose private security details publicly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or delete comments, close issues, lock discussions, block users, or take other reasonable action to keep the project usable and safe.

## Reporting

For conduct problems, contact a maintainer through GitHub. For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md` and do not post exploit details in a public issue.
